Sexy Scarred Heroes/ Beautiful Scarred Heroines

Hi, so this list will be the the sexy scarred heroes and beautifully scarred heroines. The heroines/heroes can either be physically, emotionally, or mentally scarred. Their significant other will love them despite their flaws or disabilities. The books in this list will be romance (YA NA A). Accepts all ages and sexualities. Thanks guys :)

Scarred Consist Of:
•mental problems
•anger issues
•abuse (verbal/physical)
•Disability
•War wounds/ fighting wounds
•Low Self-Esteem
•Lonely
•Can't Love
•Damaged

P.S.
Accepts Paranormal, SyFy, Contemporary, Historical, Regency, Future, etc.
